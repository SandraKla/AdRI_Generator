This Shiny App is a generator to create age-dependent data from biomarkers. Available are following distributions: Normal-Distribution, Lognormal-Distribution, Box-Cox Cole and Green Distribution, Box-Cox t-Distribution and Box-Cox Power Exponential Distribution. The parameters μ, σ, ν and τ are changing over the time with a linear or an exponentially function. The linear function is: y = m*x + b and the exponentially y = a*e^(x*b). Data can be also generated with given reference intervals for this the data from the publication "Age- and sex-specific dynamics in 22 hematologic and biochemical analytes from birth to adolescence." (Zierk, 2015) is used for Hemoglobin.

Version and Developer
